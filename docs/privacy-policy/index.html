Privacy Policy for Kertotaulutalli

Effective Date: November 23, 2024

Introduction

Kertotaulutalli ("the App") is a multiplication practice game developed by Jaakko Roponen. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The App:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT require user accounts or registration
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking
  • Does NOT access your contacts, location, or other device data
  • Does NOT contain advertisements
  • Does NOT connect to the internet
  • Does NOT share any information with third parties

Local Storage

The App runs entirely on your device. Any game progress or scores are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

This App is designed for children to practice multiplication. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Sound Effects

The App uses locally stored sound files (horse sounds) that play during gameplay. These are stored within the app and do not require internet access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document with an updated effective date.
